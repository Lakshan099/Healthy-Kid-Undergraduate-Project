App Features

IoT based child health care system for early detection of children suffering from malnutrition and other diseases and educating parents to use proper health practices. This system enables parents to take actions to promote child health using a single system that advises on the future health status of the child.

Provide signup and login features to users

Easy to install using any smartphone and providing high security to secure data

Add multiple child profiles to check health status

For parents with more than one child can add multiple child profiles to check health status

Users can easily view child health reports

Information about children's health development can be easily monitored and view health reports

Notify durations of medication and dietary patterns

Provide instructions to parents to use proper health practices and advice on the future health status of the child

Early detection of children suffering from malnutrition or other diseases

Early detection of diseases in children directly uses AI neural networks and provides quick and effective information

Provide the option to change the language to English or Sinhala

Available in three languages Sinhala, English and Tamil
